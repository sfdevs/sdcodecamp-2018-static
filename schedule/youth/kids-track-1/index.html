Younger kids workshop

Recommended for 5th grade and younger.

This workshop will focus on using Ozobot’s and Sphero’s to navigate mazes that the kids will make. We will use a combination of manual control and programming to make the bots run the maze. This will be a free running workshop that will last the entire morning (instead of the normal 50 minute sessions).

This will be a parent + kid track, where parents are expected to attend with their child.
