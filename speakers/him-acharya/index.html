Him Acharya

Development Manager / CHR Solutions

Bio:

Him Acharya is a Development Manager at CHR Solutions in Sioux Falls where he owns the development team for one of the core product. He has been in the software industry for more than 10 years and is always open to learn new techs and share it with others. His past work involved graduate programmer, software developer, Development lead and now the dev manager.

Linked-in url: https://www.linkedin.com/in/himacharya/

Session:

Career Pathing a Developer

Stop passing on life changing opportunities just because you do not know where it could end.

Many developers are asking “what’s next after software development?”. Many developers pass on opportunities because they do know about the challenges or are fully content with what they are doing.

If you are a developer that is wondering about what’s next after software development in your career path, then this session is for you. In addition, if you are content as a developer, this session will help you understand the other roles better so you could reconsider or appreciate the other roles.

We’ll talk about the paths that these opportunities could take, the responsibilities and problem-solving skills that are necessary. I’ll help you set some correct expectations for this new venture so you can succeed and enjoy it.
